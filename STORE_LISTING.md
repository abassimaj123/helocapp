## English

HELOC Calculator US

### Short Description (max 80 chars)

### Long Description (max 4000 chars)
HELOC Calculator US is the only bilingual HELOC calculator on Android that fully models both the draw period and the repayment period. If you have a home equity line of credit — or are considering one — this app gives you the complete financial picture that most calculators miss entirely.

**What you can calculate:**
- Interest-only payments during the draw period — see what you owe each month while you draw
- Full repayment period payments after the draw period closes — the principal + interest phase
- Total interest paid across both periods combined
- Complete amortization schedule for the repayment phase
- Impact of drawing different amounts — compare partial vs. full draw scenarios
- Total cost of borrowing over the entire HELOC lifecycle

**Why HELOC Calculator US is different:**
A HELOC has two completely distinct financial phases. Most generic loan calculators ignore the draw period entirely and just model a standard loan. This app models both phases accurately — showing you interest-only payments during the draw period and then the real repayment shock when the repayment phase begins. That "payment shock" is one of the most common financial surprises HELOC borrowers face, and this app eliminates it.

Combined with full English and Spanish support, this is the only tool built specifically for bilingual homeowners evaluating a HELOC — a segment with significant home equity and historically limited access to clear financial tools.

**Key features:**
- Full draw period modeling — interest-only payment calculation for any draw amount and rate
- Full repayment period modeling — principal + interest payments over the repayment term
- Combined amortization table for the repayment phase
- Total interest across both periods clearly displayed
- Partial vs. full draw comparison — see how drawing less saves money
- Bilingual: English and Spanish — full interface switch
- Export complete HELOC analysis to PDF and share
- Save scenarios to history and compare HELOC offers
- No account required, no internet — 100% local and private

**Who this is for:**
Homeowners with an existing HELOC who want to understand their repayment timeline, homeowners considering a HELOC for renovation or debt consolidation, and Spanish-speaking homeowners evaluating home equity products for the first time.

**Privacy:** All calculations run locally on your device. No personal or financial data is ever transmitted or stored outside your phone.

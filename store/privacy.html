Privacy Policy — HELOC Calculator

Last updated: April 30, 2026

HELOC Calculator ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable law including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Usage data: anonymous analytics events (e.g., screen views, calculations performed) collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash reports: anonymous crash diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Advertising data: non-personalized ad impressions via Google AdMob (free users only).
  • Purchase data: in-app purchase receipts processed by Google Play.
  • Local storage: your calculation history is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect your name, email address, home address, or any financial account numbers.

2. How We Use Data

  • To improve app stability and performance.
  • To understand which features are most used.
  • To serve non-personalized ads to free users.
  • To process your in-app purchase.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Play Billing: play.google.com/about/play-terms

4. Your CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: abassimaj@gmail.com

5. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months in Firebase. Local calculation history is stored on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
